Is it time to walk Fido?

Yes, it is!

Some pups love nothing better than a hot sunny day, while others would trade it in a heartbeat for a long romp in the snow. But whatever your pup’s preference, there’s no getting around the fact that certain temperatures just aren’t safe for outings with your dog. In very hot weather, dogs are vulnerable to heatstroke and dehydration. And at the opposite end of the spectrum, extreme cold can cause hypothermia, frostbite, and injuries.

Be sure to check the weather before walking!
